FAQ

Common Questions

Is this right for me if I have zero sales background?
Absolutely. The program is designed to meet participants at their current level. Whether you are brand-new to selling or a seasoned rep, the frameworks and drills adapt to your context. Many of our most transformed participants came in with no formal sales training at all.
What is the difference between the 3-Day and 8-Week formats?
The 3-Day Intensive is a full-cohort immersion ideal for corporate offsites, accelerator programs, or individuals who want maximum impact in minimum time. The 8-Week Immersive spreads learning across weekly live sessions with async assignments and coaching — allowing for real-time application in your actual business context.
Can Success Academy run this exclusively for our company team?
Yes. The Corporate Team Package is designed for exclusive in-house cohorts of 10–30. The program is customized to your industry, products, customer profiles, and performance challenges. We also provide a manager debrief and 30-day follow-up report.
What AI tools are covered in the curriculum?
Module 4 covers AI-powered lead generation and marketing automation tools including ChatGPT for content creation, Clay and Apollo for prospecting, CRM automation, and Meta Ads AI. Participants leave with a functional AI marketing stack setup — not just theory.

Is there post-program support?
Yes. All participants receive access to the 30-Day Revenue Sprint Group, alumni portal with templates and recorded sessions, and an annual MSNM Summit invitation. Optional monthly group coaching retainers with Mack are available at a separate investment.
