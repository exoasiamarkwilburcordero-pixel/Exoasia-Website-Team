The Five Stages

Each stage has one clear deliverable. You never move forward without it.

◎
STAGE 1
ASSESS
AI Readiness Assessment
"Know where you stand before you run."

Conduct a full diagnostic of the MSME's current state: operations, technology, team, and business model. Identify AI adoption gaps, quick wins, and transformation priority zones.

⏱ Week 1–2
KEY ACTIVITIES
Business Model Canvas review
Digital Maturity Audit (people, process, tools)
Pain Point & Bottleneck Mapping
AI Opportunity Scoring Matrix
Data Readiness Check
Financial capacity for AI investment
🎯
STAGE OUTPUT
AI Readiness Report + Priority Heatmap
◈
STAGE 2
STRATEGIZE
Transformation Roadmap
"Design the path before you walk it."

Co-create a 90-day to 12-month AI transformation plan aligned to the MSME's revenue goals, team capacity, and industry context. Prioritize by impact vs. effort.

⏱ Week 2–3
KEY ACTIVITIES
AI Use Case Prioritization (Impact–Effort Matrix)
90-Day Quick Wins Roadmap
12-Month Transformation Timeline
Budget & Resource Planning
KPI Framework (AI-specific ROI metrics)
Change Management Plan
🎯
STAGE OUTPUT
MSME AI Transformation Roadmap (Board-ready)
⬡
STAGE 3
TRANSFORM
AI Solution Integration
"Stack the right tools. Wire them right."

Design and implement the MSME's AI-powered technology stack — from CRM automation and content AI to operations and customer service — using no-code, low-code, and AI-native tools.

⏱ Week 3–8
KEY ACTIVITIES
AI Tool Selection by Business Function
Workflow Automation Design (Make.com / Zapier)
CRM & Marketing Automation (GCF / HubSpot)
AI Content Engine Setup
AI Customer Service / Chatbot Deployment
Integration & API Connections
🎯
STAGE OUTPUT
Deployed AI Stack + Automation Flows
◬
STAGE 4
REACH
Operationalize & Train
"AI only works if people work with it."

Embed AI into the MSME's daily operations. Train the team on new workflows, establish Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs), and build a culture of AI-first execution.

⏱ Week 6–10
KEY ACTIVITIES
AI Tool Training (by role)
SOP Documentation for AI Workflows
Weekly AI Sprint Cadence Setup
Prompt Library Creation
Data Hygiene & Governance Basics
AI Champion Designation within team
🎯
STAGE OUTPUT
AI-Embedded Operations Manual + Trained Team
✦
STAGE 5
AMPLIFY
Scale & Grow Exponentially
"From surviving to leading your category."

Measure, optimize, and scale. Use AI-generated insights to make faster decisions, expand to new markets, launch new products, and position the MSME as a digitally-transformed, AI-powered business.

⏱ Month 3–12+
KEY ACTIVITIES
AI Performance Dashboard (KPI Tracking)
Monthly ROI Review & Rebalancing
New Revenue Stream Identification via AI
Market Expansion Strategy (ASEAN-ready)
EBELI Growth Experiments (MTP activation)
Ecosystem & Partnership Development
🎯
STAGE OUTPUT
Scaled AI-Powered MSME + Growth Flywheel
