A crucible doesn't just apply heat. It generates something entirely new from what goes in. Every Generative Leader-Coach™ emerges not just with skills — but with a completely upgraded identity as a leader who builds leaders.

The Crucible of Generative Leaders™  ·  Core Philosophy

The Five Pillars

The Architecture of a
Generative Leader-Coach™

Every module, session, and tool in CGL is grounded in five uncompromising convictions about what it takes to truly coach a leader.

Pillar 01 · Modules 1–2
Self Mastery
You cannot take a leader any deeper than you have gone yourself. The Crucible begins with your own interior work — fortitude, presence, and emotional regulation.
Pillar 02 · Modules 3–4
Bias Towards Action
Coaching that moves. Every session must generate momentum, decision, and committed next steps — not just insight with no follow-through.
Pillar 03 · Modules 5–6
Experiments
Leaders learn by doing. Generative coaches design laboratories — safe, bounded experiments where real growth happens through action, not theory.
Pillar 04 · Modules 7–8
Grit
Real transformation is non-linear. The generative coach holds space through resistance, regression, and the messy middle — without rescuing.
Pillar 05 · Modules 9–10
Facilitating Self Efficacy & Generation
The ultimate outcome: leaders who believe in themselves and generate growth, belief, and capability in everyone around them.

The 10 Modules

A Rigorous Curriculum.
Immediately Deployable Skills.

Each module develops you across Being (who you are as a coach), Knowing (what you understand), and Doing (what you execute immediately).

Module 01
Self Mastery
The Generative Leader-Coach Identity
"Before you can coach anyone, you must know who you are as a coach."
Radical examination of your coaching identity, calling, and deeply personal motivations. Establish your Coaching Identity Statement and personal coaching philosophy.
Week 1 · 3 SessionsCIS Framework
Module 02
Self Mastery
The Interior Condition
"The quality of your coaching is limited by the quality of your inner life."
Deep dive into the BEINGness of the coach — fortitude, presence, and emotional regulation. Develop your daily Interior Condition Practice.
Week 2 · 3 SessionsPresence Dial™
Module 03
Bias Towards Action
Radical Intentionality & Coaching Architecture
"Great coaching isn't accidental — it's designed with radical intent."
Design coaching engagements with radical intentionality. Set contracts and architect relationships that consistently generate results from first session to final milestone.
Week 3 · 3 SessionsSession Design Canvas™
Module 04
Bias Towards Action
Coaching for Momentum & Decision Mastery
"The measure of a session is not the insight — it is the action it generates."
Master momentum-creation tools and decision acceleration techniques. Every engagement produces measurable behavioral change — not just insightful conversation.
Week 4 · 3 SessionsMomentum Map
Module 05
Experiments
The Experimental Coaching Lab
"Coaching is a laboratory. Leaders learn by doing — not by watching."
First live coaching lab. Participants placed in triads — coach, coachee, observer — to practice with actual leaders. Field experiments begin. Fail forward. Learn faster.
Weeks 5–6 · 4 SessionsCGL Lab Protocol
Module 06
Experiments
Narrative Coaching & Generative Questioning
"Stories are how leaders make sense of their world. Generative coaches rewrite them."
Drawing from Dr. David Drake's Narrative Coaching — use a leader's stories, metaphors, and language as primary coaching material to generate new possibilities.
Week 7 · 3 SessionsGenerative Question Bank
Module 07
Grit
The TERC Transformation Model
"Real transformation is not comfortable. And it rarely happens in a straight line."
Mack Comandante's proprietary TERC Model — Think, Emote, Respond, Create — as the core OS for coaching leaders through deep, identity-level change.
Week 8 · 3 SessionsTERC Transformation Wheel
Module 08
Grit
Coaching Through Resistance & the Messy Middle
"The greatest test of a generative coach is holding space through the breakdown."
Stay present and generative when clients face resistance, regression, and plateau. Advanced tools for defensive behavior, ambivalence, and crisis moments — without rescuing.
Week 9 · 3 SessionsResistance Nav Toolkit
Module 09
Self Efficacy
Facilitating Self-Efficacy in Leaders
"The highest coaching outcome is a leader who no longer needs you."
Grounded in Bandura's Self-Efficacy Theory — systematically build a leader's belief in their own capability through mastery experiences, vicarious modeling, and social persuasion.
Week 10 · 3 Sessions4 Levers Framework
Module 10 · Capstone
Generation · Capstone
The Generative Coaching Ecosystem
"A Generative Leader-Coach doesn't just change individuals — they change the system."
Design coaching cultures, facilitate generative team conversations, build peer networks, present your Signature Coaching Framework, and complete the CGL Certification Assessment.
Weeks 11–12 · 4 SessionsCulture Index · Cert Panel
