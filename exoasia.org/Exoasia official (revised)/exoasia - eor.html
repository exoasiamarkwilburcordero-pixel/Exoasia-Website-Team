FAQ

Questions Answered
Directly.

Who legally employs the worker?

Exoasia is the legal employer of record. We sign the employment contract under our registered entity. You direct the day-to-day work. All statutory obligations sit with Exoasia.

Is the talent application really free?

Yes. Talent applicants never pay any fee to join the Exoasia Talent Pool, apply for roles, or receive support. Revenue comes exclusively from the employer side.

Who owns the IP and work product?

IP assignment clauses in every employment contract ensure all work product belongs to the employer-client. Exoasia retains zero rights to any work performed.

Can we convert an EOR employee to direct hire later?

Yes. If you establish your own entity, we support a smooth employment transfer with zero disruption to the employee's continuity and benefits history.

How does payroll billing work?

One monthly invoice: employee gross salary + employer statutory contributions + Exoasia service fee. You remit via wire or online transfer — we handle all disbursements and government filings.

What roles can be hired through Exoasia EOR?

Any remote-ready role — tech, finance, marketing, operations, customer success, executive, and more. All hires work 100% from home. Exoasia handles the employment contract, payroll, and compliance so your team member is protected and paid correctly from Day 1.
