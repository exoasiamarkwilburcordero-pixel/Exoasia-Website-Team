Program Structure

12 Weeks.
Three Acts.

Weeks 1–3
Orientation

Immersion & Onboarding

Deep-dive into Exoasia's ecosystem, brand, tools, and methodology. Get assigned to your track, meet your mentor, and complete your first mini-project briefing.

Weeks 4–9
Execution

Live Project Contribution

Contribute to active Exoasia engagements in your chosen track. Weekly check-ins, co-working sessions, and milestone reviews keep momentum high.

Weeks 10–11
Capstone

Capstone Project Build

Develop and present a capstone deliverable that demonstrates your track expertise — a strategy report, product prototype, event plan, or AI tool.

Week 12
Graduation

Demo Day & Certification

Present your capstone to the Exoasia leadership team and network. Receive your certificate, feedback, and next-step guidance for your career or venture journey.
