The Challenge

The First 90 Days Is the Highest-Stakes
Window in a Leader's Career.

Most leaders don't fail because of lack of competence. They fail because nobody prepared them for the invisible rules of leadership at the next level.

60%
of newly promoted leaders underperform in their first 18 months — not due to skill, but due to presence and influence gaps.
40%
of executive transitions result in perceived failure within two years, costing organizations 3–5× the leader's annual salary.
90
days is the window in which leaders set the relational and reputational foundation that shapes the next 3 years of their tenure.
3×
faster time-to-effectiveness for leaders who enter with a structured stakeholder engagement and credibility strategy.
The Identity Trap
Most leaders carry their old role identity into the new one — remaining the brilliant contributor instead of becoming the strategic leader the role demands.
The Influence Gap
Senior roles require results through alignment, not command. Without a deliberate influence strategy, leaders stall on even their highest-priority agendas.
The Team Performance Lag
Without intentional team operating design, new leaders inherit broken rhythms — leading to missed targets, talent attrition, and lost credibility.
The Visibility Deficit
At higher levels, being competent in private is not enough. Leaders must claim authority publicly — in boardrooms, town halls, and executive conversations.

Program Architecture

How the ELA™
Is Delivered

A seamless blend of live cohort learning, private coaching, and peer accountability — every element bridging insight with real-world leadership impact.

Live Cohort Sessions
7 facilitated modules delivered bi-weekly via live video. Cohort capped at 18 for high-engagement dialogue and personalized interaction with every participant.
1:1 Executive Coaching
3 private coaching sessions per participant — at Days 10, 45, and 85 — anchored to real-time transition challenges and personal leadership goals.
Field Assignments
Between-session action labs applying each module directly to your real stakeholder environment. You arrive at each session with live work already done.
Peer Accountability Pods
Triads meeting weekly for 30-minute structured peer coaching using the GROW model. Accountability, challenge, and insight from true equals.
Digital Playbook Access
All 28+ frameworks, templates, and tools in a branded digital workbook — yours to keep and apply long after Day 90.
90-Day Review Ceremony
Final group session where participants present their leadership impact narrative and receive structured feedback from peers and faculty.
